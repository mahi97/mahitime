MahiTime and MahiCalendar

MahiTime

MahiTime introduces a new unit of time:

  • MahiSecond: 0.431105536 seconds
  • MahiMinute: 100 MahiSeconds (~43 seconds)
  • MahiHour: 100 MahiMinutes (~71 minutes, 51 seconds)
  • MahiDay: 20 MahiHours (~57 seconds longer than a sidereal day)
  • MahiYear: 366 MahiDays (exactly one tropical year, no leap years)

MahiCalendar

The MahiCalendar provides a structured approach to seasons and months:

  • Seasons:
    • Spring: 93 MahiDays
    • Summer: 93 MahiDays
    • Autumn: 90 MahiDays
    • Winter: 90 MahiDays
  • Months:
    • Spring/Summer: 31 MahiDays each month
    • Autumn/Winter: 30 MahiDays each month
  • Weeks: 10 days each
